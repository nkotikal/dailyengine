🇰🇷Korean Practice
과정 (gwajeong) — process, course [noun]
↳ 결과보다 과정이 더 중요합니다. = The process is more important than the result.
결과 (gyeolgwa) — result [noun]
↳ 시험 결과가 내일 나옵니다. = The exam results come out tomorrow.
이유 (iyu) — reason [noun]
↳ 회사를 그만둔 이유가 뭐예요? = What is the reason you quit the company?
목적 (mokjeok) — purpose, goal [noun]
↳ 이 여행의 목적은 휴식입니다. = The purpose of this trip is rest.
능력 (neungnyeok) — ability [noun]
↳ 그는 외국어 능력이 뛰어납니다. = He has outstanding foreign language ability.
🔹 -기 때문에 — because (of) — states a formal cause or reason; attaches to verb/adjective stems (and to nouns as 이기 때문에)
↳ 비가 오기 때문에 경기가 취소되었습니다. = Because it is raining, the match was canceled.
💡 Tip: Both -기 때문에 and -아/어서 mean 'because,' but -기 때문에 is more formal and emphatic, and unlike -아/어서 it can attach to past tense (했기 때문에) and is used in writing and explanations. Remember it cannot be used in commands or suggestions.
